## The formalities of getting an Insurance Claim

The paper you signed when you got an insurance policy for your property contained a lot of information that you deemed unnecessary.

The agent of the company should've told you that they cover all sorts of damage from molds to riots. They were correct.

However, they might have skipped the part about proving the damages. You can only get an insurance claim if the damages are proven.

The contract says that it is your responsibility to prove the damages.

Now you can prove them on your own (Good Luck!) or the insurance company can refer you to someone who can do that for you (or for them).
<br/>
<br/>

## How can a public adjuster help

At the time of getting an insurance claim, you need to do two things.

- You or someone on your behalf must prove that the damage is actually done to the insured building.
- The particular type of damage must be covered by the insurance policy you have.

Here's how you can get help! A public adjuster Bridgeton service provider will prove the damage on your behalf and will find out whether or not that damage is covered.

As a result, you will get compensation for your losses.
