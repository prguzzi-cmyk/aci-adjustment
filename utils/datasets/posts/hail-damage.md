- **Put a Tarp on It!** - ACI will help prevent further damage to your roof, siding, windows and interior of your home by putting tarps over any openings that were created by wind or hail. Missing shingles and siding leave your home exposed and often lead to interior water damage.

- **Call ACI Adjustment Group Immediately** - When it comes to assessing damage, taking pictures, and contacting the insurance company, the sooner the better. ACI will file and log your claim completely from start to finish.

- **Document Everything** - Pictures are crucial. Photographs are the best proof in documenting the loss that occurred to your home and are solid evidence that the insurance companies will ask for. How long was the hail storm? How big were the hailstones? What items were damaged inside and outside of your home?

- **Save all Receipts** - Some insurance companies withhold a portion of the settlement until all work is completed. This is known as “Recoverable Depreciation,” so save all receipts for any repairs, work, or maintenance done.

- **Let the Public Adjuster do the Talking!** - Your insurance company’s adjuster will try to minimize the claim based on what you say.
