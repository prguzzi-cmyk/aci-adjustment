Water damage is one of the leading causes of property damage.

As a matter of fact, the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), has recognized water damage as the most frequently claimed damage in property insurance.

Of all the parts of a property, kitchens are the most vulnerable for water damage.

This is, in part, because the leftover food that is washed down into the drain can clog the drain. Clogged drains are the chief cause of water damage.
<br/>
<br/>

## Types Of Water Damage

Not all cases of water damage to the kitchen are the same. Water damage can be caused by any of the following reasons:

- Overflow
- Flood
- Accidental discharge of water
- Sewer backflow
- Storm or rain
  <br/>
  <br/>

## Water Damage to The Kitchen is NOT Always Covered

One point needs to be understood here.

Even if your insurance policy covers water damage, it might not cover the damage caused by all of the above given reasons.

In order to get an insurance claim for water damage to the kitchen, your policy must cover the cause of water damage you have suffered.

Before you can get a claim for water damage to your kitchen, you need to know whether or not:

- Water leakage is covered by your insurance.
- Accidental water discharge is covered.
- Gradual water damage over time is covered.
