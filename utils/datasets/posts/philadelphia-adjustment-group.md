## Why Do You Need Philadelphia Adjustment Group?

The property insurance system of the US is designed in such a way that you need someone on your side when presenting an insurance claim case.

According to the law, if your insured property is damaged by anything that's covered under your insurance policy, it is your responsibility to prove that the damage is actually sustained by the property.

The insurance company generally refers people to a claim inspector of their own. These people are on the insurer's payroll, and they tend to produce an estimate in favor of the insurance company. `

Consequently, you might end up getting far lesser compensation than your losses.

A Public Adjuster is an independent operative who can prove the damages done to your property to the insurance company on your behalf.

As a result, you can get as much as 700% more compensation from your insurance company compared to what you'd get without a public adjuster.

## Is Hiring Philadelphia Adjustment Group Costly?

No, it is not costly per se. You do not have to pay anything upfront and state laws even prohibit the public adjusters from charging any fee before a settlement is agreed between the homeowner and the insurance company.

The fee is mostly charged in the form of a contingency fee which is up to 20% of the claim amount. Keeping in mind that you can get up to 7 times more compensation with a public adjuster on your side, that fee is anything but steep.
