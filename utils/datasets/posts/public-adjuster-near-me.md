## How Does Property Insurance Work?

When your insured property gets damaged, you go to the insurance provider. They ask you to prove that the damages have actually been done to your property before you can get any compensation.

Then they'll refer you to someone who will prove the damages. Everything looks to be working fine, right? But, there's a catch!

The person you've been referred to is on the insurance provider's payroll! They'll naturally tend to produce an estimate that might be lower than the amount required to fix your property.

As a result, homeowners in America face one or both of the following problems:

- It can take up to **several months** to get the insurance claim check.
- The money they get can be as low as **one-seventh** of the original losses.
